Colorado Teen Xtreme

Colorado Teen Xtreme is an exhilarating conference hosted by Landmark Baptist Church in Grand Junction, Colorado every winter. Immerse yourself in the thrill of our very own Colorado Classic Basketball and Volleyball Tournament, a day at Powderhorn Ski Resort, and Banana’s Fun Park. Experience the electrifying services that feature crazy games, magnificent singing, and life-changing preaching. CTX is youth conference—like never before!
